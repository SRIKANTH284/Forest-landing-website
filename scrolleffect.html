Parallax Scrolling Effects

If you're familiar with computer games from the late 80's and early 90's, you probably remember the delightful visual effects that were common to many games of that era. In games such as Super Mario Bros., the main background moves at different speeds as his character moves across the screen, creating the illusion of depth and dimension. This technique is called parallax effect. Parallax scrolling relies on different layers of content and background moving at different speeds, creating an optical illusion for the user. Parallax effect is used in many areas today, including responsive web design.
